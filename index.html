Music, Shows & Artistic Journey

Hailing from New Delhi, India, Srijan Bhattarai has carved a name for himself in the Nepali entertainment scene as a dancer, actor, choreographer, and model. With training in contemporary, hip-hop, ballet, and Bollywood dance styles, he brings versatility and passion to every performance. His journey took a major turn in Nepal when he joined the second season of Himalayan Roadies, reaching the top 10 and winning the hearts of fans and judges alike. This opened doors to music videos, modeling, and choreography opportunities, allowing him to showcase his artistic range. Srijan has also made a mark on Nepali television, notably in shows like Aina (2021) and The Poet Ideal (2023), blending acting and performance to create memorable experiences for audiences. He continues to expand his craft, recently starring in music videos with popular Nepali artists like Satya Raj Acharya. From participating in pageants like Mrs. & Miss Supranational Nepal to consistently pushing creative boundaries, Srijan Bhattarai exemplifies dedication, versatility, and passion—a true multitalented artist inspiring the next generation.
